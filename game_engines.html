Game Engines

This page is to give a short explanation as to each of the game engines and their respective languages. Click an engine to go to it's website!


Unreal Engine 4

One of the most well known game engines, Unreal Engine 4 is a game engine that bases it's game coding through C++ and visual scripting known as "Blueprints". This engine has a wealth of tutorials available everywhere on the internet, and is capable of creating simple games, through to complex games. This engine is best known for the popular battle royale game Fortnite.

Unity

Unity is also one of the top game engines, with graphical prowess and varied capabilities it easily squares up against UE4, it's primary coding language is C#, and has plenty of available tutorials on the internet. This engine is likely best known for titles such as Escape from Tarkov, and Ori and the Blind Forest.

Cryengine

Likely one of the most graphically capable, publicly available game engine, Cryengine is built by developers Crytek, and has a primary coding language of C++. It is best known for games such as Crysis and Far Cry.
